◆ About Me

A builder at heart, learning to teach machines to think.

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science & Technology (KNUST), Ghana, on a mission to become an AI/ML Engineer. My journey started with building robots and hacking into systems (ethically!) — now I'm channeling that same curiosity into deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

I believe AI should solve real problems, especially for communities that need it most. Whether it's precision agriculture, healthcare access, or smart infrastructure — I want to build intelligent systems that make a tangible difference, starting right here in Africa.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me tinkering with Arduino circuits, reading about AI safety, or mentoring younger students in robotics and cybersecurity.
